Based on collective work and shared knowledge, Nuuk aims to favor dialogue and debate, to transform individual knowledge into increased creative potential.

The company principle of Architecture-Studio is the collective conc eption. From the very beginning, the practice has believed in the virtues of exchange, crossing ideas, common effort, shred knowledge.
